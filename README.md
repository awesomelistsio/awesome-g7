> A curated list of resources, reports, data, and policy tools related to the Group of Seven (G7) — a forum of seven of the world's most advanced economies.

The G7 consists of Canada, France, Germany, Italy, Japan, the United Kingdom, and the United States. It addresses global issues such as economic policy, climate change, health security, and technology governance.

## Official G7 Resources

- **[G7 Official Website (UK Presidency)](https://www.g7uk.org/)** – Past presidency website with documents and news (check each presidency for more current info).
- **[G7 Japan 2023](https://www.g7hiroshima.go.jp/)** – Official website of Japan’s 2023 presidency.
- **[G7 Germany 2022](https://www.g7germany.de/g7-en)** – Official site from Germany’s presidency in 2022.
- **[G7 Research Group – University of Toronto](https://www.g7.utoronto.ca/)** – Independent source for G7 research, communiqués, and summit analysis.

## Communiqués & Summits

- **[G7 Communiqués Archive](https://www.g7.utoronto.ca/summit/index.html)** – Complete archive of official communiqués and declarations.
- **[G7 Summit Statements](https://www.consilium.europa.eu/en/meetings/international-summit/)** – Statements issued during summits and ministerial meetings.
- **[G7 Global Health Summits](https://www.who.int/news-room/events/detail/2021/05/21/default-calendar/g20-global-health-summit)** – Joint G7/G20 health initiatives (linked through WHO and other agencies).

## Data & Policy Dashboards

- **[OECD G7 Data Dashboard](https://www.oecd.org/g20/topics/oecd-and-the-g20.htm)** – Policy comparisons and data indicators relevant to G7 and G20 countries.
- **[IMF Data Portal](https://data.imf.org/)** – Economic data across G7 countries with filters for GDP, debt, inflation, and more.
- **[World Bank G7 Overview](https://data.worldbank.org/)** – Economic and development data for each G7 nation.

## Research & Reports

- **[Brookings – G7 Insights](https://www.brookings.edu/tag/g7/)** – Expert analysis of G7 outcomes, decisions, and policy themes.
- **[Chatham House – G7 and Global Governance](https://www.chathamhouse.org/topics/g7)** – Research on G7 priorities and geopolitical influence.
- **[Carnegie Endowment – G7 Commentary](https://carnegieendowment.org/)** – Think tank commentary on G7 diplomacy, security, and trade.
- **[European Council on Foreign Relations](https://ecfr.eu/)** – Coverage and position papers related to G7 and multilateral strategy.

## Policy Themes

### Climate and Energy

- **[G7 Climate and Energy Ministerial Documents](https://www.g7germany.de/resource/blob/997532/2042894/13ba49d8ff889d7b98280b6211e34648/2022-05-27-g7-climate-energy-communique-data.pdf)** – Joint communiqués on global climate action and clean energy investments.
- **[G7 Just Energy Transition Partnerships](https://www.climatechangenews.com/2022/06/28/g7-just-energy-transition-plan-emerging-economies/)** – Agreements with countries like South Africa and Indonesia.

### Global Health

- **[G7 Global Health Initiatives](https://www.g7.utoronto.ca/health/index.html)** – Coordinated pandemic response, vaccine access, and WHO reform.

### Digital & Tech

- **[G7 Digital Ministers' Declaration](https://www.bmwi.de/Redaktion/EN/Downloads/digital-ministers-declaration-g7.pdf)** – Joint statements on tech policy, AI, and cross-border data governance.
- **[G7 Cyber Norms](https://www.lawfaremedia.org/article/2021-g7-communique-cybersecurity)** – Cybersecurity, encryption, and trust frameworks.

### 🕊Democracy & Security

- **[G7 Responses to Global Conflict](https://www.whitehouse.gov/briefing-room/statements-releases/2022/03/24/g7-leaders-statement/)** – Statements on the Russia-Ukraine war and security partnerships.

## Related Groups & Forums

- **[G20](https://g20.org/)** – Broader group of global economies with overlapping goals.
- **[OECD](https://www.oecd.org/)** – Think tank and policy coordination body involving all G7 members.
- **[World Economic Forum – G7](https://www.weforum.org/agenda/archive/g7/)** – Strategic insights and business perspectives on G7 topics.

- **[IMF API](https://datahelpdesk.imf.org/knowledgebase/articles/667681-how-to-use-the-imf-data-api)** – Global economic stats across G7 nations.
